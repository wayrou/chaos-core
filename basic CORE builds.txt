Medical Ward
Role: casualty control, recovery, stabilization
Requirements to be online:
50crates of supply
50watts of power
Strategic function
Gives post-battle healing to squads operating within 100bw comms of connected range
Tactical battle tie-in
Squads within 100bw comms are granted health recovery post-battle.
Incapacitated units can only be revived when their squad reaches a Medical Ward.
Network identity
Works best when supplied and with strong comms. 
a good “rear line” C.O.R.E., not usually ideal on the frontier unless the player is sustaining a forward push
Failure / damage consequences
prolonged campaigns become riskier 

Armory
Role: ammunition, combat readiness, munitions support
Requirements to be online:
100crates of supply
50watts of power
Strategic function
improves readiness for connected squads
Tactical battle tie-in
Connected battles within 100bw comms start with an attack buff, improving opening offensive tempo.
Network identity
Needs heavy resource connection
Failure / damage consequences
offensive momentum falls apart

Command Center
Role: local control, coordination, threat response
Requirements to be online:
50crates of supply
100watts of power
Strategic function
Improves comms in connected rooms, taking an input source of >1bw and outputting it to 100bw of comms (as long as it has the 100watts power to be online)
Tactical battle tie-in
Connected battles within 100bw comms start with a defense buff, improving opening offensive tempo.
Network identity
Requires heavy power network
ideal for chokepoints, intersections, and high-value sectors
Failure / damage consequences
redeploy and response options get worse
nearby network becomes less stable and less informed

Mine 
Role: extraction, raw material generation
Requirements to be online:
50crates of supply
50watts of power
Strategic function
generates a steady trickle of metal and timber per tick.
good long-term value but weaker immediate battle support
Tactical tie-in
Improves post-battle resource gain in connected rooms with 50bw comms.
Network identity
wants secure adjacency and protection
not usually a frontline C.O.R.E. unless the player is gambling
best in sectors with natural resource tags
Failure / damage consequences
income drops
construction plans become harder to sustain
the player feels long-term economic pain instead of immediate battle pain

Refinery
Role: Just like mine, but generates steam per tick.
Requirements to be online:
50crates of supply
100watts of power
Tactical tie-in
Improves post-battle resource gain in connected rooms with 50bw comms.
Supply depot
Role: logistics anchor, resource distribution
Requirements to be online:
50crates of supply
50watts of power
Strategic function
Improves supply in connected rooms, taking an input source of >1crates and outputting it to 100crates of supply (as long as it has the 100watts power to be online)
Tactical tie-in
Squad battles within 50bw of comms gives the player gear and consumable rewards post-battle.
Network identity
one of the most foundational C.O.R.E.s
defines the backbone of a push
often worth protecting over flashier buildings
Failure / damage consequences
downstream rooms become cut off
multiple other C.O.R.E.s may weaken at once
one lost Depot can cause a cascading logistics collapse
